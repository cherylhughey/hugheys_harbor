What should you bring?

The beauty of the campsite is its remote location. This means limited accommodations are available for sleeping. Be sure to bring a tent, sleeping bags and whatever else will keep you comfortable with the weather forecast. We will supply all the kitchen equipment, but you'll want to bring food and water. A portable toilet is available for your use and showers are in driving distance. An extra pair of shoes is always advised.
